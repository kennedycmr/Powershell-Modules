Cmdlet category: Cf

Disable-NaCluster
Disables the takeover capability of this filer in the cluster.
Enable-NaCluster
Enables the takeover capability of this filer in the cluster.
Get-NaCluster
Get useful information about the status of the clustering service.
Get-NaClusterHwAssist
Get useful information about the status of hw_assist functionality.
Get-NaClusterHwAssistStats
Get useful information about statistics of hardware assisted takeover functionality.
Get-NaClusterPartner
Get the host name of the partner.
Invoke-NaClusterForceTakeover
Forces one filer to take over its partner even though the filer detects an error that would otherwise prevent a takeover.
Invoke-NaClusterGiveback
Initiates a giveback of partner resources.
Invoke-NaClusterTakeover
Initiates a takeover of the partner.
