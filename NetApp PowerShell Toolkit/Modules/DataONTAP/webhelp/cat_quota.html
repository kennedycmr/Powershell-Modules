Cmdlet category: Quota

Add-NaQuota
Adds a quota entry.
Disable-NaVolQuota
Turns the quota subsystem off for a volume.
Enable-NaVolQuota
Starts to turn quotas on for a volume.
Get-NaQuota
Obtains a quota entry specified by type, target, volume, and tree.
Get-NaQuotaEntry
Get the list of quota entries in /etc/quotas.
Get-NaQuotaReport
Generates a report on quotas.
Get-NaVolQuotaStatus
Obtains the status of quotas for a volume.
Remove-NaQuota
Deletes a quota entry specified by type, target, volume, and tree.
Set-NaQuota
Sets a quota entry.
Start-NaVolQuotaResize
Starts an ONTAP operation to resize quotas for a volume.
