Cmdlet: Get-NcGpoRestrictedGroupApplied


NAME

SYNOPSIS

    <NcController[]>] [-ZapiRetryCount <Int32>] [<CommonParameters>]
    
    Get-NcGpoRestrictedGroupApplied -Template [-VserverContext <String>]
